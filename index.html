Membership Terms

1. Eligibility

Any individual aged 18 years or above, or any legal entity registered in Bangladesh, is eligible to become a member of Unity MicroFund upon completing the registration process and satisfying the KYC requirements.

2. Registration Process

To become a member, applicants must:

  • Complete the online registration form with accurate information
  • Submit valid government-issued identification (NID/Passport)
  • Provide proof of address
  • Verify email address and phone number
  • Accept and agree to the Terms of Service

3. Member Obligations

Members must:

  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date profile information
  • Not share login credentials with others
  • Report any suspicious activities immediately
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

4. Account Security

Members are responsible for maintaining the security of their account and shall be liable for any activities conducted through their account. Two-factor authentication is available and recommended.

5. Termination

Unity MicroFund reserves the right to terminate or suspend any membership that violates these terms or engages in fraudulent activities.

Investment Agreement

1. Investment Process

Investors can browse available projects and invest funds through the following process:

  • Select a project from the active listings
  • Choose investment amount (minimum varies by project)
  • Complete payment via designated channels
  • Receive investment confirmation and certificate

2. Profit Distribution

Profits are distributed according to Shariah-compliant profit-sharing agreements:

  • Profits are calculated project-wise based on actual returns
  • Distribution occurs quarterly or as per project terms
  • Profit is credited to member wallet accounts
  • Members can reinvest or withdraw profits

3. Risk Disclosure

All investments carry inherent risks. Past performance does not guarantee future returns. Members should carefully review each project before investing.

4. Investment Limits

Each project has a maximum investment limit per member as specified in the project details. Total investment exposure is monitored for risk management.

5. Documentation

All investment agreements are documented digitally and can be accessed from the member dashboard at any time.

Withdrawal Policy

1. Withdrawal Eligibility

Members can withdraw their invested funds and profits subject to the following conditions:

  • Investment term has been completed
  • Project has been closed and final settlements made
  • No pending obligations or disputes

2. Withdrawal Process

To withdraw funds:

  • Submit withdrawal request from dashboard
  • Select withdrawal method (bank transfer/bKash)
  • Provide verified bank account details
  • Processing takes 3-7 business days

3. Withdrawal Fees

A nominal processing fee may apply for withdrawals.Refer to the fee schedule for current rates.

4. Timing

Withdrawal requests are processed within 7 business days. Peak periods may experience slight delays.

5. Restrictions

Withdrawal may be temporarily restricted during ongoing project investments or during regulatory investigations.

Share Transfer Policy

1. Transfer Eligibility

Investment shares can be transferred to other Unity MicroFund members under certain conditions:

  • Both parties must be verified members
  • Investment term must allow transfer
  • No outstanding disputes on the investment

2. Transfer Process

To transfer shares:

  • Initiate transfer from investment dashboard
  • Provide recipient member ID
  • Recipient must accept the transfer
  • Transfer completes after confirmation

3. Transfer Fees

A transfer processing fee applies as per the current fee schedule. This covers administrative costs.

4. Restrictions

Certain investments may have transfer restrictions specified in their terms. Not all projects allow intermediate transfers.

5. Documentation

All transfers are documented and a digital certificate is provided to both parties for records.
